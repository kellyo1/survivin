Advice: How To Life

Cleaning and Tiding

Fold your clothes straight out of the tumble dryer (if you use one), while they're still warm. This minimises creases and eliminates the need for ironing.

Money

Use a money tracker like mint,(check out our app sections) Enter all expenses asap! (You will forget if you don't!) And just keep in mind your expenses in general!

Organization

Set three main goals each day, with one of them being your #1 priority. Don't overload your to-do list or you'll hit overload paralysis and procrastinate.
