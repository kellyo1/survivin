Survivin'

YOU'RE BEING CHASED BY AN ANIMAL! AAAH! BUT DON'T PANIC!

Firstly remain clam! Now depending on the animal you have a few options. One option would be play dead. Limit your breathing, remain perfectly still and seemingly lifeless. Another option would be make yourself appear bigger, outstretch your arms, stand on tiptoes, ext. This is make you seem like a bigger animal thus making the animal give up the chase. Lastly one option(in my honest opinion the best option) would be just to keep running! Run for your life! Keep going until safety is for sure!
